FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Account

Do I need to create an account to

browse the services?
No, it is not necessary to create an account to mainly browse. However, you will be needing an account to complete your transaction and create a reservation.

Can I register in behalf

of my organization?
Yes, you may but the application asks for your contact details such as your name details. Make sure you have a representative in behalf of your organization.

How can I retrieve my password?
You can retrieve your password by clicking “forgot password?”. Instruction on password retrieval will be send to your email.

How do I change my personal

details or email address?
You can edit your personal details by navigating "Account" > and access your details.
 Reservation

How can I reserve?
When you find a service you need, you can add it to cart, and go through the reservation process. After the desired reservation is ready, you will receive an invoice to your email. This will also be stored to your account.

What payment methods can I use?
We do not handle payments online. After the scheduled return of your rented equipment, you will be billed accordingly.

How can I retrieve my password?
You can retrieve your password by clicking “forgot password?”. Instruction on password retrieval will be send to your email.

How can I see the availability?
All the equipment and service which are shown on our app are available. Reservation lead time depends on the products and quantities.
 Customer Support

How can I contact customer service?
On pricing and shipping related issues you can contact customer support at hytest@hytest.fi or call +358-2-512-0900. It is also available on the Contact Us page.

Can I make an inquiry without

logging in?
Yes, you can but the application asks for your personal details to customize the response. Otherwise, you can access FAQs.
